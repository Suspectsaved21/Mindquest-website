🧠 MindQuest Support

Welcome to the official support page for MindQuest: The Knowledge Adventure! If you're experiencing any issues, have questions, or want to send us feedback, you're in the right place.

💬 Common Issues

  • ❓ The app crashes or freezes
    Try closing the app and restarting your device.
    Ensure you're using the latest version from the App Store.
    Still not working? Reach out to us below 👇
  • 🔇 Sound isn’t working
    Make sure your device is not on silent.
    Open Control Center and check media volume.
  • 🧩 Trivia or content is missing
    Check your internet connection.
    Some content may unlock after progress.
